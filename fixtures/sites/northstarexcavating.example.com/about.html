About Northstar Excavating

Founded in 1998, Northstar Excavating has grown from a single backhoe to three full-time crews and a support team of 22 employees.

Dale Hendrickson, Owner, started the company after fifteen years operating equipment for regional contractors. His son Erik Hendrickson serves as Operations Manager and coordinates daily dispatch for all crews.

We are independently owned and locally operated. We work directly with commercial general contractors and municipal public works departments across northeastern Minnesota.

Our office team schedules jobs by phone and text us anytime for urgent site questions — crews check in with the office every morning.
